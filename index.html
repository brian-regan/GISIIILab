Zürich Air

Ever wondered about air quality in Zürich? This map shows the PM 2.5 measurements for a small section of central Zürich roadway, collected using a SenseBox, alongside traffic data for the city allowing you to examine the relationship between vehicular traffic and air quality.

EPA Victoria states "PM2.5 particles are smaller than 2.5 micrometres in diameter. They are up to 30 times smaller than the width of a human hair". They often occur from burning of fossil fuels and other materials.

"Children, people over 65, pregnant women and people with existing heart or lung conditions (including asthma) are more sensitive to the effects of breathing in fine particles".
